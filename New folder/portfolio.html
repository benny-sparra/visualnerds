What We Make

Types of AI Film Work

Here's the kind of work we take on. Each project is bespoke — tell us what you need and we'll tell you honestly if we can deliver it.

🎬

Short Films

Narrative short films generated with AI — from 2 minutes to feature length. Ideal for festival submissions, experimental projects, and creative showcases.

📺

Commercials

Brand and product commercials that use AI-generated visuals to create look-and-feel that would be impossible or cost-prohibitive with traditional production.

🎵

Music Videos

Visually arresting AI-generated music videos that match the energy, mood, and identity of your track — without a single camera on set.

🏢

Brand Stories

Origin stories, vision films, and brand identity pieces that build emotional connection between your audience and what you stand for.

✨

Visual Effects

AI VFX integrated into existing footage — scene extensions, environment replacement, de-ageing, crowd simulation, and generative compositing.

🔬

Experimental Work

We love unusual briefs. If you have an idea that doesn't fit a category above, come talk to us — the stranger the better.
