Watch Out for Impersonation

If you receive an email claiming to be from VisualNerds but it comes from a different domain (e.g. visualnerds.co.uk, visualnerds.net, visual-nerds.com, or any free email provider), it is not from us. Please do not click any links, make any payments, or share personal information in response.

Forward suspicious emails to support@visualnerds.com and we'll look into it.
